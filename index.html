COMPANY BACKGROUND

ArBee Solar

Is an electrical contracting company with expertise in Solar PV System Installation, Design & Engineering, Repair and Maintenance services. We also offer wide variety of services in the field of electrical engineering including building electrical installations, supply of electrical equipment such as transformers, MDP, ECB, MTS, ATS and others. We also offer CCTV surveillance, solar streetlights and solar pumps for irrigation and water supply systems.

ArBee Solar was established to make solar energy accessible to every Filipino households and businesses. We advocate for sustainable and renewable energy with an attractive rate of return. Since its inception, our steadfast goal is to be the standard promoter of sustainable and renewable energy and expedite transition to a greener and cleaner alternative energy source in the country. We aim to encourage households, businesses and public and private entities to make a decisive switch to renewable energy to save the environment, reduce their electricity cost and have supplementary power during power outages and energy crisis.

MISSION

To power-up every households and businesses through cost-effective, high quality, environment friendly, sustainable and renewable solar energy

VISION

To become a leader in large scale solar installation, sales, and maintenance services. We envision the Philippines to be the top solar consumer in the years to come
